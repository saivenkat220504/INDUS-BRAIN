PLANT FACILITY #04 - UNIT ALPHA MAINTENANCE PROTOCOL
Document Date: 2026-07-22
Author: Lead Plant Engineer Alex Morgan

1. EQUIPMENT SPECIFICATIONS
- Primary Pump: PUMP-A-102 (Centrifugal High Pressure)
- Safety Valve: VALV-V-804 (Set point: 18.0 Bar)
- Steam Turbine: TURB-C-301 (Operating speed: 3600 RPM)
- Gas Compressor: COMP-K-09

2. REGULATORY & COMPLIANCE STANDARDS
All operating pressure testing must comply with OSHA 1910 General Industry standards and ASME Boiler Code specifications.

3. FAILURE MODES & RISK PREVENTION
- PUMP-A-102: Susceptible to cavitation when inlet pressure drops below 1.2 Bar.
- VALV-V-804: Inspect for overpressure thermal stress and valve seizure during steam purge.

4. LOCATION & BAY ASSIGNMENT
Equipment located in Unit Alpha, Control Room Bay 4, Turbine Hall.
